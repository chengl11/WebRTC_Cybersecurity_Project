Thank you for installing WebRTC Notifier!

Why You Need WebRTC Notifier
  1. Chrome does not notify user of the usage of WebRTC, except for asking for microphone and camera permission.
  2. WebRTC may be abused. It might leak your IP address behind VPN, result in network congestion, or be used for malicious activities.
Features of WebRTC Notifier
  1. Notify users about the usage of WebRTC;
  2. Display details about WebRTC like chrome://webrtc-internals/.
Privacy Policy
All work is done in user's device. We never get or keep user's information.
